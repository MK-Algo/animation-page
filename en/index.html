Do you know the scent of incense made solely from natural fragrance ingredients?

Most incense available on the market today contains synthetic fragrances and colorants.
Is it really harmless to the human body, even though it's possible to obtain a strong fragrance at a low cost?

At Kuyuri and Wolf Moon, we are committed to "100% natural" and use only carefully selected high-quality materials.
Our incense master, "Koushi," creates each piece with great care, from blending to completion.

This incense is extremely rare, made entirely from natural ingredients without using any synthetic fragrances.
Please enjoy the authentic fragrance of traditional Japanese incense and experience the true power it possesses.

I n c e n s e 　M a s t e r
K o u s h i

As the name suggests, one who "governs" the "fragrance".

It refers to the artisan who, based on their accumulated experience, honed sense of smell and intuition, and pride, takes full responsibility for everything related to incense.

To create the ideal fragrance from many fragrance raw materials, the artisan conducts the entire process from blending to completion.

The incense master is also a traditional Japanese profession that dates back to the Edo period.

T h r e a t 　o f
S y n t h e t i c 　F r a g r a n c e s

In modern times, there is a concern known as "fragrance harm".

Fragrance harm refers to various health issues triggered by synthetic fragrances (chemical substances) found in items such as synthetic detergents, fabric softeners, and cosmetics.

Some individuals develop chemical sensitivity as a result of this fragrance harm.
This condition causes them to react sensitively to even small amounts of substances, which can accumulate in the body over time. Incense is not an exception to this.

At "Kuyuri and Wolf Moon," we believe that the fragrances entering the body through the nose should be beneficial for both the body and mind.
That's why we are committed to using only natural materials.

C a r e f u l l y 　S e l e c t e d
F r a g r a n t 　M a t e r i a l s

At Kuyuri and Wolf Moon, we use only the finest quality of Aloeswood, known for its deep yet light and sweet fragrance, among various types of sandalwood.
We also select rare natural materials such as Taniboku and Siamese Agarwood.

Fragrance has five tastes, and all of our incense is carefully blended by the incense master, who thoughtfully considers the balance of these five tastes.

Please enjoy the unique characteristics of each aromatic material, such as the soft, exquisite sweetness of Siamese Agarwood that gently envelops you, and the rich sweetness of Taniboku, which subtly carries hints of spiciness and bitterness.

At "Kuyuri and Wolf Moon," we are committed to using only natural materials because we want the fragrances that enter the body through the nose, which are often overlooked, to be beneficial for both the body and mind.
